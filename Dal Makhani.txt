Recepe for making Dal Makhani

1)Take 1 bowl of black udid dal and 1/4 bowl of rajma and let soak for one night.

2)Next day boil rajma and black udid.

3)Give a tadka in butter with jeera,mustered,turmeric,chilly powder and garam masala.

4)Then make paste of 3 tomatoes and add it to the tadka.

5)Mix well.

6)Add boiled rajma and black udid and cook well.

7)Add some cream on top and you're tasty and yummy dal makni is ready to searve.
